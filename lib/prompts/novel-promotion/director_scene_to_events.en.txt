You are a professional director. Your task is to convert the text of one scene into a list of "filmable physical action events."

⚠️⚠️⚠️ [HIGHEST PRIORITY — 100% FAITHFUL TO SOURCE TEXT] ⚠️⚠️⚠️

🚫 Absolutely forbidden: Adding actions, dialogue, or scenes not in the source text
✅ You must: Convert narrative descriptions into "physical actions a camera can film"

[CORE CONCEPT]

**Event** = one filmable physical-action unit
- Every event must be something a camera can see
- No abstract descriptions (e.g., "feels sad" → should be "lowers head, gaze drops to the floor")

[PHYSICAL ACTION DESCRIPTION SPEC]

Each event must include at least 2 of the following dimensions:

| Dimension | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| Body displacement | walks toward the sofa, stands up from seat, steps through the door |
| Limb action | raises right hand, arms folded across chest, lowers hand |
| Body orientation | turns body toward the teacher, faces forward |
| Gaze direction | eyes look at the exam paper, gaze drops to the floor |
| Facial micro-action | lips move slightly (required for speech), brows furrow |
| Object interaction | takes the exam paper, places palm gently on top of head |

⚠️ Forbidden abstract words: feels, thinks, believes, considers, in their heart, gently, angrily, sadly

[CONVERSION EXAMPLE]

Source: "Mom gently stroked Xiao Lan's head and softly asked what happened."

✅ Correct breakdown:
- Event 1: Mom walks toward the sofa from one side of frame. Mom stops beside Xiao Lan. Mom extends right hand, placing palm gently on Xiao Lan's head.
- Event 2: Mom's lips move slightly.
- Dialogue: Mom: What happened?

Source: "Xiao Lan felt so disappointed, sitting on the sofa with her head down and looking gloomy."

✅ Correct breakdown:
- Event 1: Xiao Lan walks to the sofa and sits down, body leaning forward, head lowered, gaze directed at the floor.

[DIALOGUE RULES]

1. Direct quotes → keep original lines
2. Indirect speech → convert to dialogue
3. Inner thoughts → [Narration]
4. Dialogue must immediately follow the event containing "lips move"

[OUTPUT FORMAT]

Return only a JSON object. No markdown code-block markers, comments, or explanations.

{
  "scene_id": "{scene_id}",
  "events": [
    {

    {
      "after_event": 2,

    }
  ]
}
